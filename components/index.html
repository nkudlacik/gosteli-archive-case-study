Mission Statement

The Gosteli Archive collects and makes accessible sources on women's history. We advise researchers on their research and share our knowledge with a broad audience.

Women Together

What have women fought for and achieved together? We tell success stories of the women's movements in Switzerland: the recognition of unpaid care work in the old-age and survivors' insurance (AHV), greater social security for female farmers, participation in sports, and much more.
